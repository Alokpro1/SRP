Kereal Flood


Time flows by, oscillating between the good and the bad. As we live, God's own country is suffering God's wrath. The beautiful palm-lined beaches, the soul stirring network of canals and the eye-catching mountain slopes lie crying, as Kerala is hit with one of the most destructive floods of recent times.
To save God's own country from this unexpected wrath of God, we, as God's own creation, should come forward in this hour of peril and need. For saving millions, we billions must open our arms and uphold the Incredibility of India.



Aasra Diya Project



Decorated with colors and love by the underprivileged children from AASRA workplaces, AASRA diyas shine like true gems in the night. AASRA provides the diyas and raw material, and the delicate hands of the children make their fire shine brighter. With each diya you buy, you award their creativity and help them towards a better future. So, come, light a dream this Diwali and wash away the hazy shade of winter.




As the Earth needs organs more than heaven.



The world's riches and beauty can be witnessed or savored to the fullest only with the help of our vital organs. Unfortunately, not each and every mortal being on the face of this globe is blessed with all of them. Organ donation to help the needy is like bringing light into darkness, variety into monotony and euphoria into tediousness. Innovision'18 requests you to promote and take part in such activities in order to spread warm fuzzies and make the world a better place!
